The Thesis

The complete capital stack for the space economy.

Venture capital gets a space company off the ground. It is the wrong tool once the company has hardware on orbit producing revenue. The missing tools — debt, infrastructure, insurance — have matured every other capital-intensive industry before this one. Building them for space is what we do.

Thought Leadership

The next space inflection point is financial — not technical.

"The success of the space economy will not be written by venture capital alone. It will be written by those who build the complete capital stack — the equity that sparks ideas, the credit that scales them, the infrastructure that grounds them, and the insurance that de-risks them."

Florida's Space Coast launched 109 times in 2025. Revenue-generating assets are proliferating on orbit. But the financing architecture required to scale proven space companies into cash-flow-positive operators has not kept pace with the launch manifest.

In this white paper, Meagan Crawford — one of the leading voices in space venture — lays out the four-layer framework being assembled at Ramsey Financial Group, and what governments, investors, and founders need to build together now.
